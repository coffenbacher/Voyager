About

Voyager is the SCOPE script collaboration site for BingAds.

Tips:

  • You're encouraged to both post and answer your own question when you figure something out.
  • To post a tutorial, please phrase it as a question. Post the question, then post your tutorial as the answer and accept it.
  • Voyager works best for non-transient questions. E.g. it's not a good place to be asking about an ongoing live site, but is a good place to ask about historical idiosyncracies.
  • I'll periodically send Kudos to the most helpful contributors, so management knows how much you're helping the rest of us!
